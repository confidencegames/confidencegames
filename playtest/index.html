Get Involved! Playtesters needed.

Many eyes make all bugs shallow

Game design is one of those hobbies that just needs a constant stream of objective feedback. I do my absolute best to make every game bug-free. But as you get close to any project, you need an outsider's viewpoint.

As a professor by day, I know the value of solid feedback. My usual day usually involves some sort of peer review: writing and revising publications, peer-reviewing journal articles papers, grading student work, writing grant proposals. I know that, in any creative endeavor, you get close to it. You need objectivity. You need both big picture feedback and nit picking.

So, I need your help.
