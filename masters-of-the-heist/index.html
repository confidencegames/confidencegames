Make Money. Pull Heists.

In this head-to-head match, you must compete to hire a team of professional criminals, plan heists on unsuspecting marks, and muscle out your opponent to earn money. It's all about return on investment and knowing your opponent.

The game plays in a series of 5-7 rounds, each round with three phases: The Purchase, The Plan, and The Heist. The player with the most money at the end wins.

The Purchase. Spend Wisely.

Hire your Crew to pull your heists. We've got 22 Crew cards, each with unique bonuses and abilities.

Your team's possibilities are endless. Grifters are highly skilled and get you to those tougher marks. Partners are better at collaborating. Thieves get you fast cash. Muscle is great for getting the Mark you want. Insiders will specialize for Marks in various neighborhoods.

The Plan. Choose Carefully.

Plan your heists by secretly sorting your cards in your hand. Prioritize your choices of Mark, too.

For example, maybe you want your Pickpocket to go after the Sketchy Bar and you want your Fall Guy to hit the Honest Bank.

Place your crew face-down when ready.

The Heist. Plans unfold.

Plans are put in motion that cannot be changed! You attempt your planned heists, but if you run into your opponent you must rely on your best Muscle to save you.

The Heist phase is entirely mechanical, but exciting. Once you get paid, you collect your take and head back to The Purchase phase. Newer, tougher Marks become available, too.
